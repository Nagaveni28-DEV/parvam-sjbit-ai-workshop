1. ))Zero Shot Prompt:
Classify the sentiment of this flipkart user review: 
“Received product in the morning, It was great but delivery got delayed”


Result :
The sentiment is Mixed (Neutral).

Reason:

Positive part: “It was great” → shows satisfaction with the product
Negative part: “delivery got delayed” → shows dissatisfaction with service

So overall, the review contains both positive and negative opinions, making it a mixed sentiment.
