----Task on Answering Question with Evidence:
The Eiffel Tower was completed in 1889 for the World's Fair. It is 330 meters tall. Over 7 million people visit it each year. It was the world's tallest structure until the Chrysler Building was completed in 1930.
Using only the text above, answer: "What year did the Eiffel Tower stop being the world's tallest structure?"  
Then quote the exact sentence that supports your answer.

Result:---
Answer: 1930

Evidence: “It was the world's tallest structure until the Chrysler Building was completed in 1930.”
